### üîπ What Is a Response-Time Analysis?

A short report you create for a prospect that shows:

- How long it takes their business to respond to a missed call
- How that compares to industry benchmarks
- How much revenue they're likely losing

**This is your #1 sales weapon.** It turns a cold prospect into a warm one because the data is about *their* business.

### üîπ How to Run the Analysis

**Step 1 ‚Äî Call the prospect after hours (3 times over 1 week)**

| **Call #** | **Day / Time** | **Result** | **Response Time** | **Response Type** |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 1 | e.g. Mon 6:30 PM | Voicemail, no callback | Never | None |
| 2 | e.g. Wed 7:15 PM | Voicemail, callback next day | 14 hours | Phone call |
| 3 | e.g. Sat 10:00 AM | No answer, no response | Never | None |

**Step 2 ‚Äî Calculate their average response time**

- If they never responded: *"Your average response time to after-hours calls is: No response"*
- If they responded once out of 3: *"You responded to 33% of test calls with an average delay of 14 hours"*

**Step 3 ‚Äî Compare to benchmarks**

| **Response Time** | **Lead Outcome** |
| --- | --- |
| **Under 1 minute** | Best-in-class ‚Äî lead is highly likely to convert |
| **1‚Äì5 minutes** | Good ‚Äî 80% higher conversion vs. 30+ minutes |
| **5‚Äì30 minutes** | Risky ‚Äî lead is already shopping competitors |
| **30+ minutes or never** | Lead is lost ‚Äî 80% have already called someone else |

**Step 4 ‚Äî Estimate revenue impact**
