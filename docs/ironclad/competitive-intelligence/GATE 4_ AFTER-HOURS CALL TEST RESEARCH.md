## Introduction

For home-service contractors, every missed call represents a potential lost job and damage to their reputation. This is especially true for after-hours calls, which often come from customers with urgent needs. This report examines the after-hours call handling practices of home-service contractors, with a specific focus on the Minneapolis / Hennepin County, MN market. The research covers industry-wide data on missed calls and response times, an investigation into local complaints, and an analysis of competitor response times. The findings highlight a significant opportunity for IronClad's AI-powered missed-call capture service to provide a competitive edge.
